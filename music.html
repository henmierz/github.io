Galena Crew

Based out of Chicago, IL — Galena Crew is comprised of childhood best friends Henry, Maksym & Tuzzy. After nearly 8 years, Galena Crew has gone from a niche hobby to a forceful movement.

About

Music through the lens of 3 childhood best friends

Being part of “the Crew” is more than just inclusion, it's about sharing the energy and happiness we have on stage and in the studio.

We began making music together in college with nothing but a laptop and a dream. Nearly 8 years later, we've played some of the biggest festivals across the US and shared stages with artists like The Chainsmokers, Dillon Francis, and Two Friends.
