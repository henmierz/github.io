About Galena Crew

Music through the lens of 3 childhood best friends

Being part of "the Crew" is more than just inclusion — it's about sharing the energy and happiness we have on stage and in the studio. From basement sessions in Chicago to festival stages across the country, every track carries the same DNA: raw friendship and unfiltered energy.

Henry, Maksym, and Tuzzy began making music together in high school with nothing but a laptop and a dream. Nearly a decade later, they've shared stages with The Chainsmokers, Dillon Francis, and Said the Sky — and the momentum shows no signs of slowing.
